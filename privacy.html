6. Third-Party Services

7. Data Removal

Users may request data removal by contacting the Bot developer or by removing the Bot from their server, where applicable.

8. Children’s Privacy

The Bot does not knowingly collect personal information from users under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Bot after changes implies acceptance of the updated policy.
